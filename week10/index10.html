Questions:

  1. I'm a little confused on what exactly guard properties are and what they are used for?

Answers:

  1. Guard properties are useful because they are intended to minimize the effect that hackers can have on the headers. If they are smart enough, hackers can try to modify the headers to either retrieve or change information so it can be useful to them, perhaps stealing personal information, or stealing server information. The guards help safeguard against many of those attacks.
